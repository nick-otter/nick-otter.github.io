**whoami?**

I am interested in the dull side of technology: servers, switches, uptime, the OSI model. I think it's great to empower individuals to enjoy working on these things and I believe in the [Dead Internet Theory (DIT)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dead_Internet_theory).

**Why Anni Albers?**

Anni Albers was a German-Jewish visual artist and printmaker. A leading textile artist of the 20th century, she is credited with blurring the lines between traditional craft and art - which is also a communal aim for many pretentious engineers.
